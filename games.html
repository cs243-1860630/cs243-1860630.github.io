Me as a gamer

I have been an enthusiastic gamer since kindergarten(yes kindergarten, when I used to
play games made by flash). I play a wide variety of games, but RPG games and strategy
games have always been my favorite. Here I am going to introduce several of my
favorite video games. Just for fun.

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

tags: Open World, RPG, Story-Rich, Atmospheric

I have many games in my Steam Library, but I have only finished a few of them. The
Witcher 3 is a game that I did not only one, but more than 5 playthroughs. The story
is so intriguing that I could not stop thinking about it even during classes (umm that
is probably not good...).

Rating: ★★★★★ (5 stars)

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

tags: Open World, RPG, Fantasy, Adventure, Action

This is by far the best open world RPG game in the world (in my opinion). Unlike the
Witcher 3, where the RPG part plays a more important role, Skyrim emphasizes on the
the open world element of the game, meaning instead of experiencing a story written
by someone else, you get to write your own story. You can be a hero, a thief, an
assassin, or just a normal people. Everything is totally up to you. It also has an
active mod community, which keeps adding new contents to the game.

Rating: ★★★★★ (5 stars)

Europa Universalis IV

tags: Grand Strategy, Strategy, Historical, Simulation

This game allows you to play as one of the nations existed in the world from 1444 to
1821. It is a little bit complicated to get started with this game because there are
so many systems and mechanics. However, once you are familiar with it, there will be
an infinite number of possibilities to explore. You can play as Spain to discover and
colonize the new world, play as the newly founded United States to fight for
independence, or even create your own country and try to conquer the world. Of course,
my favorite thing to do is always to REBUILD THE ROMAN EMPIRE! S!P!Q!R!

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4 stars)
